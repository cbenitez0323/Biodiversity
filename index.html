The human body is home to many bacteria ans different parts of the body harbor different species of bacteria. There is also a difference in taking samples from the same area of the body but from two different people. A study was conducted where people had their bellybuttons swabbed in order to identify the range of bacteria that was found. Here, the results have been displayed. Each participant has an id number which will then display the results of the sample id and the values of those samples, meaning the number of bacteria grown. The gauge will show the frequency of which they washed their bellybutton weekly. Finally, the bubble graph displays all the samples at once, where each bubble is a different bacteria species and the size of the bubble is how much of that bacteria grew.
